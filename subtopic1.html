What is Virtual Reality?

URL

Virtual Reality

Author

Britannica

Abstract

Virtual reality uses computer modeling and simulation to allow a person to interact within an artificial, three-dimensional environment. VR immerses users in an artificial environment that simulate reality through interactive devices like goggles, headsets, gloves, or body suits that send and receive information. The purpose of the article is to inform of what VR and what the history of VR is.

What is VR

Author

Strivr

Abstract

Virtual reality (VR) is a technology that lets people experience a digital world using a headset. When someone wears the headset, they can look around and feel like they are in a different place. The images and sounds make it seem real, even though it is not. VR is used for games, learning, and job training. For example, workers can practice tasks in a safe way, and people can learn new skills. It is useful because people can repeat activities and learn by doing. Overall, VR is becoming more popular and is used in many parts of life.
