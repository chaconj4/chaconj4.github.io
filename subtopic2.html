Different Types of Virtual Reality

URL What Are the Types of VR?
Author Scene3D
Abstract There are many different forms of virtual reality. While there are many forms, there are 4 main forms that dominate virtual reality: non-immersive, semi-immersive, fully-immersive, collaborative, and augmented virtual reality.

URL The 3 Types of Virtual Reality
Author Heizenrader
Abstract Virtual reality simulates human senses such as vision, hearing, and sometimes touch, allowing users to feel like they are inside a different environment. Through this technology, a computer acts as a gateway to a new world where users can interact with digital surroundings. The quality of a VR experience depends on the available content and the power of the technology being used. There are three main types of virtual reality simulations used today: non-immersive, semi-immersive, and fully immersive systems.
